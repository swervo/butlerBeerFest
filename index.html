☞ Hello and welcome to the 1st Butler Beer Festival

If you have heard of this festival then you are among the hand-picked few selected for their deep knowledge and love of ale in all its guises, and their convivial nature.

☞ ☞ Beer festival ale

All of the ales available at this festival are locally sourced - from Tescos.
However there are a couple of guest ales from the Bitter End as well. These will be carefully selected on the day to ensure maximum freshness and originality.

☞ ☞ Food

Alongside the delicious ales locally sourced produce will also be available. A range of delicious sausages and handmade breads will be available throughout the day and at very reasonable prices.

☞ ☞ Entertainment

Live music will be provided by hereisfinn who, in case you haven't heard of her, is an up and coming singer songwriter who has already collected rave reviews and a large and growing fan base.

In addition the inaugural pretzel making competition will also be taking place some time during the day.

So come along and be merry!
